Research Interests

🧠

Neuroimaging

Leveraging structural and functional MRI to map the developing human brain with high spatial resolution and precision.

👶

Developmental Neuroscience

Tracing neurodevelopmental trajectories from infancy to adulthood to understand how cortical organization emerges over time.

👁️

Vision Science

Characterizing category-selective regions in the ventral temporal cortex and how visual representations are functionally organized.

💭

Cognition

Investigating the neural substrates of higher-order cognitive processes and how the brain encodes abstract visual categories.
